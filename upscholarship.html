The Uttar Pradesh Government has released the UP Scholarship Online Form 2024-25 for state students, available to General, OBC, SC, and ST categories and other minority communities. Two types of scholarships are offered: UP Pre Matric Scholarship 2024 for 9th and 10th grade students, and UP Post Matric Scholarship 2024 for 11th and 12th grade students.

All students must apply online at https://scholarship.up.gov.in before the last date in 2025. The last date for post-matric scholarships will be in December 2024 for SC and ST candidates, and in January 2025 for general, OBC, and minority categories.



Eligibility Criteria to Fill Out UP Scholarship Online Form

To be eligible for the Uttar Pradesh Scholarship, students must meet the following conditions :


Domicile Requirements

  • Only students who are residents of Uttar Pradesh can apply.
  • Applicants must be enrolled in a school, college, or university within Uttar Pradesh.
  • The institution where the student is studying must be registered on the UP scholarship portal.

Academic Qualifications

  • Post-Matric Scholarship (11th and 12th Grade): Students must be in the 11th or 12th grade.
  • Post-Matric Scholarship (Beyond 12th Grade): Students must be enrolled in a recognized university or college for undergraduate or postgraduate courses.
  • Pre-Matric Scholarship: Students must be in the 9th or 10th grade.

Category Requirements

  • Students from Christian, Sikh, Bodh, Muslim, Parsi, and Jain communities can apply under the Minority category.
  • SC, ST, OBC, and General category students are eligible to apply.

Step by Step filling of UP Scholarship (For college Freshers General Students)


Step 1: Online Registration

  • Visit the official website: scholarship.up.gov.in.
  • Navigate to the 'Menu' and select 'Student' to register for a new scholarship.
  • Click on the appropriate link to apply and fill in the required details.
  • Create a secure password and submit the form.
  • Note down the application number displayed on the screen.

Step 2: Login

  • After registration, go to the official website's homepage.
  • Open the UP Scholarship 2024 login page.
  • Select the type of scholarship you are applying for and open the corresponding link.
  • Enter your registration number, date of birth, and password.
  • Complete the Captcha and submit.

Step 3: Fill Out the Application Form

  • Once logged in, access the application form link.
  • Enter the following details:
    • Educational information
    • Personal information
    • Caste and income details
    • Bank details
    • Current school/college fee details
    • Previous year's educational details
  • Click the 'Update' button to save your information. A confirmation message will appear on the screen.
